Appendix 1: Enzymatic reactions by metabolic pathway and EC number

The EC (European Commission) number is a classification system for enzymes, organized by the type of reaction catalyzed. You can use the list below to search this textbook for information about the reaction catalyzed by a given enzyme. You can also use the EC numbers to search for information in databases such as SwissProt Enzyme Nomenclature Database (http://enzyme.expasy.org) or the BRENDA Comprehensive Enzyme Information System (http://www.brenda-enzymes.org).
